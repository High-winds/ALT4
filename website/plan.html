Plan stage

We chose to make a device that could detect when patients in a hospital are in danger or have fallen. We chose this idea as it met all the goals, we wished to accomplish such as using inter-device communication and helping the less able.
Here is some of our previous ideas and their advantages and disadvadvantages:

Idea Advantages Disadvantages Accepted
Gym Progress Tracker More people are entering the new year so there is an increse in market size. Not much possibility for meeting perscribed basic and advanced requirements. Did not receve unanamus intrest so further ideas were pursued. ❌
Elderly Memory Game It will help our target audence, which makes it more meaningful for us. The project would be overly complaceted. we cannot match its features with the perscribed requirments of the project. ❌
Patient Fall and Distress Detector It meets the both the basic and advanced requirments set out by the project brief. It also helps our target audence so holds a higher importance to us. It may be dificult to measure and quantify what consttutes a fall, and differenciate it from normal perambulation ✅

The hardware required at the moment would simply be the micro:bit itself. We will also be using a breadboard to further control the input and outputs. We will be designing the software for the centeral micro:bit on the MakeCode account assigned to Jake, and the software for the detecting micro:bit on the Makecode account of Alex.

The idea is for the system to detect any falls or accidents a patient has, broadcast that information to an external breakout board to be used as a central control. This data can then be broadcast too other micro:bits that will act as receivers to the incident.
